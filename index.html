DIANA MYURI

I’m Diana Myuri, a social media creative director and brand strategist with a passion for building authentic brand identities. I specialize in creating visual identities, content strategies, and digital campaigns for fashion brands, personal accounts, and lifestyle businesses.
My expertise spans social media management, photography, video production, and social media creative direction, all designed to create meaningful, long-lasting impact.
With a focus on timeless design and strategic storytelling, I craft brand experiences that resonate deeply with audiences and deliver real results.

SOCIAL MEDIA CREATIVE DIRECTOR & BRAND STRATEGY

My approach begins with understanding a brand’s goals, which informs the strategy and visual aesthetics. This ensures brands achieve success both creatively and in business, building strong, cohesive identities that resonate with their audience. By aligning strategy with storytelling, I help brands establish a clear vision, combining creative direction with branding to create meaningful, lasting connections.

Social Media Creative Direction & Post-Production
I manage the process of social media creative director, from initial concept to final execution. This includes:
  • -Developing creative concepts and strategies tailored to the brand's objectives
  • - Curating photos and content to create a cohesive, compelling narrative.
  • -Writing campaign names and captions that reflect the brand’s unique identity.
  • - Leading post-production to ensure campaigns are polished, visually aligned, and consistent across all platforms.
Social Media Content Creation & Strategy
Social media is more than just posting — it’s about telling a story that connects with your audience. I create content that resonates with your followers and builds a lasting relationship.
  • - Developing strategic content plans that align with your brand’s identity and objectives
  • - Writing captions and managing community interaction
  • - Overseeing the aesthetic of your Instagram feed and Stories to ensure a cohesive brand image
  • - Managing the post-production process, ensuring content is polished and on-brand
Graphic Design Direction & Team Management
While I don’t create the graphics myself, I lead the graphic design direction & provide creative guidance on every visual aspect of the campaign, ensuring all elements reflect the brand’s vision.
  • -Directing the design aesthetic, including color palettes, fonts, and layouts
  • -Managing the design team to ensure deadlines are met and designs align with the strategy
  • -Reviewing and approving all final designs to ensure quality and consistency
Video Production & Reels Creation
I oversee the entire video production process, from concept to final edit, to ensure that every shot tells a compelling story and engages the audience.
  • -Conceptualizing and scripting video content to match the brand’s message
  • -Directing video shoots and managing logistics to capture key shots
  • -Selecting editing styles, speed, and design elements that enhance engagement
  • -Overseeing the final edit to ensure it’s aligned with the brand’s aesthetic and optimized for social media
Project & Team Management
In addition to my creative direction, I also oversee and manage the entire production process, from conceptualization to execution.
I coordinate with videographers, photographers, designers, and other creatives to ensure that everyone works towards the same vision. This includes building the shooting concept, selecting the right team, creating the mood board, and planning every detail of the shoot.
  • -Leading the team and managing workflows to implement the creative vision
  • -Developing and executing the shoot concept, including the creation of Mood Boards and shoot planning
  • -Managing logistics and overseeing the production process, ensuring everything runs smoothly
  • -Reviewing drafts, providing feedback, and ensuring all deliverables meet the brand’s standards

ABOUT

DIANA MYURI STUDIO

I’m Diana Myuri, a creative director and brand strategist based in Tel Aviv. I specialize in developing visual identities, content strategies, and digital campaigns for fashion brands, personal accounts, and lifestyle businesses, helping them build lasting connections with their audiences.

With a focus on timeless design and strategic storytelling, I aim to elevate each brand’s presence and ensure long-term success. My services include social media management, photography, video production, and creative direction, all customized to meet the unique needs of each brand.

Creative Approach & Philosophy

I believe in the power of thoughtful, precise design to tell compelling stories. My creative process emphasizes high-quality, timeless design, prioritizing enduring aesthetics over fleeting trends. Approaching each project as a unique journey, I carefully craft it to build meaningful connections between brands and their audiences, leaving a lasting impression.

Industry Experience & Specialization

I’ve had the pleasure of working in the fashion world with amazing brands like Ronny Kobo, Department Fashion Store, Coconut Love Jewelry, and Mother Of All. I’ve also helped clients refine their personal brands, like Eti Kobo, founder of Coconut Love, by shaping their digital identities to create stronger connections with their audience. Fashion is my main focus, but I’ve also ventured into beauty with Wow London and commercial projects with Ofer Shopping Malls. For Ofer, I manage everything from storytelling to shooting all the content they use for their stories and social media. I oversee every step of the creative process, from direction to photography, ensuring the final result truly resonates with their audience.

What Sets Diana Apart

I believe in the power of thoughtful, precise design to tell captivating stories. My creative process emphasizes high-quality, timeless design, focusing on aesthetics that stand the test of time rather than fleeting trends. For me, every project is a unique journey, crafted to build meaningful connections between a brand and its audience, leaving a lasting impression.
