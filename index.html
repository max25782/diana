ABOUT

DIANA MYURI STUDIO

Diana Myuri is a creative director and brand strategist based in Tel Aviv. With a clear vision and a focus on results, she collaborates with fashion brands, personal accounts, and lifestyle businesses to develop strategic content, visual identities, and storytelling that resonate with their target audience. Diana’s work is rooted in creativity, trust , and a commitment to delivering timeless, effective results.

Creative Approach & Philosophy

At the core of Diana's work is a partnership with her clients. She believes in understanding their vision and using her expertise to make it bolder and more impactful. For Diana, the ultimate goal is always the final result — designs and content that sell while distinguishing the brand from competitors. Diana’s approach is based on a blend of creativity and precision, where every detail matters. She strives for perfection, from crafting a visual identity to creating content that speaks directly to the audience. Each project is treated as an individual journey, ensuring value for both the client and their customers. Her creative process is grounded in timeless, high-quality designs that avoid fleeting trends, creating lasting impressions that inspire and connect audiences to the brand

Industry Experience & Specialization

Diana has worked extensively in fashion, collaborating with brands such as Ronny Kobo, Department Department Fashion, Coconut Love Jewelry, and Mother of Sun. She has also contributed to personal branding, helping clients like Eti Kobo, the founder of Coconut Love, refine their digital identities. Each project receives a tailored approach, with strategies adapted to meet the client’s unique goals — from boosting sales to building personal connections with followers. While her primary focus is on fashion, Diana has also explored the beauty sector, working with brands like Wow London, adding depth to her diverse portfolio.

What Sets Diana Apart

For Diana, communication and continuous growth are central to the creative process. By integrating the latest trends and technologies, she ensures her clients stay ahead. Her designs connect deeply with the audience's emotions, creating a lasting impact. Diana’s work reflects a commitment to minimalism and timeless quality. Every project is rooted in a deep understanding of the client’s brand, ensuring it not only stands out but also remains relevant. By combining strategic planning with creative expertise, Diana crafts visual identities that are both effective and beautiful, delivering real business results that endure.
